Here is a link to QBAtlas. Some Tips to help you get started...
1. Go to the Reader tab, update the filters, and fetch tossups (s)
2. Hit Space to buzz in, and Enter to enter your answer
3. Click the blue buttons to save clues to your database, which can be viewed in the Clues tab
4. Check the Stats and Packet Log tabs to review your performance
